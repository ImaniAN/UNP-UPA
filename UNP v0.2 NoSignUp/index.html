Frequently Asked Questions

What is United Network Partners?

United Network Partners is a Person to Person, Direct Funding and Crowd Sharing Platform. It brings forth a new way of raising funds for various causes, whether it is for personal needs or a host of worthy causes, such as churches, schools, non-profit organizations, etc

Is United Network Partners a company, business or a corporation? 

Neither, United Network Partners is a Platform made by the people and for the people. It was created by a group of like-minded people for this very purpose.

Can I have more than 1 Account?

Yes, we encourage you on having additional accounts for your different goals, but we strongly recommend that you have two members to sign up under that new account.

What Donation/Pay Processor are used?

Local Banks, VISA an MasterCard

Are payments automated?

No, as you receive donations from another participant, you will need to verify that the funds were deposited in whichever Donation/Pay processor your chose(Local Banks, VISA an MasterCard Accounts). Then and only then should you approve the donation. Donations must be approved within 12 hours. If the beneficiary does not take action within that time limit, their account will be blocked until some action is taken and during the locked timeframe, they will not be able to receive any donations; nor will they be able to donate
